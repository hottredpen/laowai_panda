Overview

Laowai Panda is the first mobile app that is exclusively aimed for expats travelling, living, working and studying in China. The app aims to provide answers to every question, problem or situation that may face a foreigner in China, whether in business, education, health or other topics. With different available interfaces, language profiles, and the ability to post a question in any language about any topic; Laowai Panda would be your best friend in China.

Philosophy

As the app is designed, programmed and presented by expats living and working in China, we believe that an in-depth idea of what is needed will be served by heart.

How it works!

Any requested question could firstly be searched about to preview the possible last answers. In the case that the question was not been asked before; a new post will start receiving different answers, and based on rating for the most useful answer in response for the presented question; anyone who posts a question will surely have a wide variety of answers to choose from.
